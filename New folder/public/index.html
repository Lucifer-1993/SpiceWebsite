From the Archives

"Imagine a dream where you’re taking a stroll in a garden full of plants with colourful foliage. You’re ravished by the enchanting view, indeed. A dream that reminds us of the heavens. Now imagine a similar experience but in reality. Saffron fields in the Kashmir valley bring a lot to the table, offering breathtaking scenery. Yes, saffron as a spice has extraordinary medicinal properties, known worldwide for its unique taste and other nutritional benefits. We, as consumers, receive the end product, the spice, but don't realize how beautiful the landscape is where the spice is grown. Let me share an anecdote: growing up my friends and I would drive to the Pampore saffron fields during the blooming seasons and run around the fields. The experience I will never forget. Last year, the government of India issued Geographical Indication (GI) tag to Kashmir-grown saffron. As a result, the spice gained a considerable market base in the international market. Simply put, the valley-grown saffron is top-class in the world, hence the GI tag. Long story short, I have launched this platform to bring you the purest form of saffron, directly from the Pampore fields. Notably, the farmers who I buy from are enthusiastic about Tarly Spices and Foods as the platform meticulously focuses on purity and quality."
